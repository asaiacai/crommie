{% include section-header.html title="Crommie Group" %}

We are a condensed matter research group located on the UC Berkeley campus and at LBNL that integrates scanned probe microscopy with other techniques to investigate the structural, electronic, optical, and magnetic behavior of low-dimensional (1D and 2D) nanostructures. Our current research areas of interest include topological and relativistic effects in low-dimensional materials, bottom-up synthesis of 1D and 2D molecular structures, corrrelation effects in 2D materials, bottom-up synthesis of 1D and 2D molecular structures, correlation effects in 2D materials (including magnetism, superconductivity, CDWs, and Mott insulator behavior), optically excited nanostructures, and molecular machines.
